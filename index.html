At Your Service

Established in the year of 2007 by an expert techno commercial head, worked for India and international textile industry for more than 30 years.

The decade passed journey is on its pace by exporting 50% of its production to more than 40 countries around and its special combed gassed mercerized yarns has amplified the width of journey to non-domestic areas. The unique captive consumption production of Indian traditional dress material by handloom constitute the 50% of our production satisfying the domestic market.

Today the Vicram's range covers cotton to Garments and the best quality Cotton Yarns produced by Indian and Imported cotton fibers including carded & combed ring spun yarns of coarse & fine counts, ply yarns and special yarns. Overwhelming demands from the markets motivated the company to invest in production line of Yarns and Fabrics. The company is synonymous as a reliable one-stop shop for yarns, fabrics and cotton.

"VISHVAS" brand Organic Cotton garments out let will be opened soon in the IT capital city Bangalore.
The Company serves markets across the world and is poised for further growth ahead under the able leadership of professional management. The dynamic feature of our company injects timely and indexed production to the market.
